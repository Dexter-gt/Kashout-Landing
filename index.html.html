How it works under the hood

Powered by USDC

Kashout uses USDC — a dollar-backed stablecoin — as the payment rail. Your clients pay in USDC, and you withdraw straight to your local bank account.

Share your link

Generate a personal Kashout payment link. Share it with any client — they pay in USDC with no account needed.

Receive USDC instantly

USDC lands in your Kashout wallet the moment payment is made. No delays, no banking intermediaries.

Withdraw to your bank

Convert USDC to local currency and send it to your bank. 1% flat fee on conversion — nothing else.

Why USDC?
USDC is always worth exactly $1. No price swings, no volatility — just a fast, stable dollar you can hold and spend with confidence.
