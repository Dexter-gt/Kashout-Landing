The Problem

International payments are broken for Africa

Existing tools weren't built for the African freelancer. They're slow, expensive, and complicated.

It's too expensive

  • High bank fees (5–15%)
  • Hidden conversion charges
  • Intermediary bank cuts
  • Unfair exchange rates

It's too slow

  • Settlements take 3–7 days
  • Wire transfer delays
  • Banking hour restrictions
  • Manual review holds

It's too complex

  • Forex restrictions
  • IBANs and SWIFT codes
  • Complex crypto setups
  • Limited payment options
